Frequently Asked Questions

If you have other questions, please contact us at support@checkrideai.com.

How does it work?

Our expert AI instructor asks you questions like the ones you'll see on your private pilot checkride. It then grades your response and points out areas of improvement.

Does this replace my CFI?

No! You'll still need your Certified Flight Instructor to prepare you for the practical portion of the exam. We help you prepare for the oral section so you can spend less time on the ground and more time in the air.

Do I get a certificate of graduation from this?

No. Our software is geared to help you ace your checkride. You'll need to work with your CFI or online ground school to get the certificate that allows you to take the private pilot written test.

Do I need to download an app?

No. Checkride AI runs in your browser, so you don't have to download or install anything.

Which pilot licenses does this work for?

Private pilot license. Instrument and commercial checkride prep coming soon.
